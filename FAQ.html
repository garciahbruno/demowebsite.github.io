Frequently Asked Questions

What is Camp AG?

Camp AG is a summer program focused on academic games for high school students. It provides a fun and educational environment to challenge students in various subjects.

How do I register?

Registration opens on March 1, 2025, and closes on April 1, 2025. You can register through our website by filling out the interest form.

Is there a cost to attend?

Camp AG is free of charge. We aim to make the program accessible to all students regardless of financial background.
