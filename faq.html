Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs
What should my child bring each day? +
All your child needs to bring is their lunch.
How can parents communicate with their child during the camp? +
The head instructor will provide you with their phone number and email in the case that you need to contact us.
What is the lottery system? +
Your child will have an equally random chance to attend camp as everyone else. It will be picked randomly.
How can my camper continue this outside of camp? +
Multiple schools in the Ann Arbor School District have after school clubs where students can learn more and compete. If you're looking to start one at your local school reach out to us! We will help.
